# Legal evidence export

`GET /v1/internal/events/{event_id}/legal-export` produces a restricted package containing original filenames and storage references, SHA-256/BLAKE3 hashes, custody events, review decisions, and immutable publication snapshots. Every export appends an audit event.

The included Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam Section 63 section is a draft schedule only. Counsel must identify the responsible person and system, verify ordinary-course and device particulars, determine admissibility, and complete signature, date, and place. The generator is not a substitute for legal advice or testimony.

Exports must be written to encrypted, access-controlled storage with an expiry or legal hold. Never place an export, evidence file, source identity, or reviewer note in GitHub.
