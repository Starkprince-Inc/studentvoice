# Privacy and retention

StudentVoice minimizes data at intake, does not retain submitter IP addresses in the domain record, encrypts optional contact details separately, and supports one-time anonymous receipts. Originals are access-controlled and never offered as public downloads.

Public derivatives must obscure bystander faces, minors, protected witnesses, vehicle plates, device metadata, medical details, and source-revealing context unless a documented public-interest exception is approved by counsel. Face detection may create redaction boxes only; embeddings, biometric matching, gait recognition, and cross-file re-identification are prohibited.

Default retention is seven years for accepted or published evidence, 30 days for rejected originals, and indefinite retention for hashes and audit receipts. Legal holds override deletion. These defaults must be converted into a counsel-approved retention schedule before production.

Data residency defaults to Azure India geography. Production access requires MFA, least privilege, short-lived grants, sensitive-action reauthentication, quarterly access reviews, and audited exports.
