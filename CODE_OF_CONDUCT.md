# Community Code of Conduct

StudentVoice contributors must protect the dignity, safety, and privacy of people represented in civic evidence. Harassment, doxxing, threats, discriminatory conduct, coordinated targeting, publication of private identities, and attempts to bypass editorial safeguards are prohibited.

Be precise, cite evidence, distinguish observation from inference, correct mistakes openly, and assume that disagreement can be documented without dehumanizing people. Maintainers may remove contributions or restrict participation when conduct threatens contributors, sources, subjects, or the integrity of the project.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers. Do not include sensitive evidence or personal data in the report unless a secure channel has been agreed.
